About Us

At PRJ Care Foundation, we are dedicated to transforming the lives of underprivileged children through education and enrichment activities. Based in New Delhi, India, our foundation provides free classes in English, Hindi, and Maths, helping over 100 kids take their first steps toward literacy. In 2023, we supported 25 children in enrolling in school for the first time and offered additional classes to help them catch up with their peers. Our weekly events, including cleanliness and plantation drives, festive celebrations, and creative activities, foster a sense of community and joy. With the help of our passionate volunteers and generous donors, we strive to empower the next generation and create a brighter future for all. Join us in making a difference today!

Our Values

At PRJ Care Foundation, our core values are rooted in upholding human dignity and leveraging the world's surplus to create positive change. We believe in:
Respecting the dignity of all individuals, including ourselves, our partners, and the communities we serve. Embracing localized solutions that harness local resources, wisdom, and efforts. Improving before changing, by valuing and exploring the potential of existing resources, and promoting circularity in all aspects. Fostering collaboration in every sphere to increase effectiveness and scale. Recognizing every entity as a stakeholder in the development process. Prioritizing the often-overlooked, including marginalized people, underutilized materials, unmet needs, neglected issues, and underserved geographies and relationships.
These values guide our actions and decisions, ensuring that we work towards a more equitable and sustainable future for all.

Our Impact

At PRJ Care Foundation, we're committed to creating a positive impact in our community and beyond. In the past year, we've made significant strides in promoting environmental sustainability, civic engagement, and healthy living. Our plantation drive saw us planting over 100 trees, helping to reduce carbon footprint and preserve biodiversity. We also launched a voting campaign, empowering 1000+ citizens to exercise their right to vote and shape the future of our democracy. Additionally, we celebrated key milestones like Yoga Day, Earth Day, and Republic Day, using these opportunities to raise awareness about the importance of physical and mental well-being, environmental conservation, and national unity. Through these initiatives, we've made a tangible difference in the lives of 2000+ people, and we're proud to continue our work towards building a better tomorrow.
