Plantation Drive

A Collective Effort for a Greener Future

PRJCARE FOUNDATION recently conducted a successful Plantation Drive, engaging interns from various institutions, including Nikunj from NMIMS and NSUT interns Shubham Chakma, Mehul, Shivam, Shubham Yadav, Aryan Negi, Nivedita, Kushagra, Uday, Yogesh Kumar, Amit Patel, Aryan Mahajan, and Aman Pandey. Under the guidance of mentors Mrs. Phoolaji and Mrs. Praveen Kalia, the drive received robust support from government co-ed school site-1, with Principal Mr. P K Pradeep and Vice Principal Mrs. Garima Gupta playing pivotal roles. The event was further bolstered by the presence of volunteers, students, Mr. Anshul (co-founder), and Mrs. Rajni Joshi (Director of PRJCARE FOUNDATION).

The Vision Behind the Plantation Drive

The Greenmask Plantation Drive Project, undertaken by PRJCARE FOUNDATION, is a dedicated initiative to preserve and enhance our environment. The project focuses on the intricate web of interconnected systems that make up our planet, including the air we breathe, the water we drink, and the ecosystems that support all life forms. By promoting tree planting and green practices, the foundation aims to address the delicate balance that sustains biodiversity, climate stability, and human well-being. This initiative is a step towards mitigating the negative impacts of human activities on the environment.

Encouraging the Youth to Take Action

One of the key aspects of the Plantation Drive was encouraging children and young people to participate in tree planting activities. Recognizing that human activities such as pollution, deforestation, and the release of greenhouse gases are causing significant harm to the environment, the foundation emphasized the importance of conservation and sustainable practices. By involving the younger generation, PRJCARE FOUNDATION aims to foster a sense of responsibility and collective action among the youth, ensuring that the efforts to protect and restore our environment continue for future generations.
